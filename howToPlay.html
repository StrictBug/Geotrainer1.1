How to Play

Single Player Mode:

  • Select an area and the number of rounds from the menu.
  • For each round, a location name is displayed, and you have 15 seconds to place a marker on the map by clicking.
  • Try to place your marker as close as possible to the actual location.
  • After each guess, the actual location is revealed, and your distance is calculated in kilometers.
  • Aim for the lowest average distance across all rounds.

Multiplayer Mode:

  • Host a Game: Enter your name, choose an area and number of rounds, and create a game to get a unique code.
  • Join a Game: Enter the game code and your name to join other players.
  • Compete with others to guess locations as accurately as possible within 15 seconds per round.

  • See everyone's scores after each round, with distances within 1000km getting points.
  • The player with the most points at the end wins!

Have fun and sharpen your geography skills!
